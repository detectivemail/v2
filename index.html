Know the myths

Detective Mr. Alexander Ivan

Russian Crimes & Forensics Branch

Intelligent, observant, serious. Used to deductions and unravelling crimes. Has grown the popularity of being the living Sherlock Holmes. Knows perfect English despite being a Russian. Has a great knowledge of firearms.

Detective Kevin Miles

New York Police Department

Funny, intelligent, young. Has a great mind but doesn’t really use it in deductions. Great at finding out new reasons for what might have been. Has a lot of ideas on modern technologies and devices. Good at reading people.

Officer McGill

Dublin Police Department

Bored, tired and exhausted from his job. But his skills keep bringing him more responsibilities for the town. And Dublin has never been this much mysterious before, and he assumes there is a link with a top criminal on all the cases.
